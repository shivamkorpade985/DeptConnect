Features

Everything your department needs

Three powerful modules designed to digitize and simplify everyday departmental workflows.

Announcements

Post role-targeted announcements. Students, faculty, and HODs see only what's relevant to them. Real-time updates with clean, card-based layouts.

Document Repository

Upload, organise, and share syllabi, notes, assignments, circulars, and forms. Cloud-powered storage with one-click downloads.

Leave Management

Students apply to their class teacher, faculty apply to the HOD. Automatic approver assignment, real-time status tracking, and one-click approvals.
